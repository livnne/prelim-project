What is 映画?

映画 (えいが, eiga) is a Japanese word that refers to "movies" or "films". When combined, "映画" refers to the art form of movies or films, where moving images are projected or reflected onto a screen. It encompasses various aspects of filmmaking, including storytelling, cinematography, acting, editing, and more. In everyday language, "映画" is used to refer to movies or films in general, regardless of their genre or origin. It's a term deeply ingrained in Japanese culture, as Japan has a rich history of filmmaking and is home to a vibrant film industry producing a wide range of works, from traditional Japanese cinema to contemporary international releases.
